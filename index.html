BAKERY

We bake fresh everyday! We offer a variety of cakes, tortes, tarts & cupcakes, along with fresh baked breads & scones. Ingredients matter, so that is why we never use artificial flavors, colors or preservatives.

CAFETERIA

Cafe Latte is unlike any restaurant you’ve ever experienced. Our award winning salads, sandwiches are made fresh right in front of you. Every day we offer more than six different soups, a dozen sandwiches & five prepared salads, all of which are ever changing. Grab a tray – you’re in for a wonderful experience!

PIZZA & WINE BAR

The pizza & wine bar, located in the back of the cafe, is the ideal place to meet friends or unwind at the end of the day. There is an extensive menu of gourmet pizzas that pair perfectly with our interesting yet approachable wine list. More than 30 + wines by the glass!
